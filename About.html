About Us

Our History

With Gelasa Shoe Store was founded in 2005 by shoe enthusiasts Jane and Mark Smith. What started as a small, family-owned business has grown into a beloved shoe emporium known for its commitment to quality and style.With a passion for footwear that's unmatched, the Smiths set out to create a shoe store that would cater to the diverse tastes and preferences of their customers. Over the years, Gelasa has become a trusted source for the latest shoe trends and classic styles.

Our Products

At Gelasa, we take pride in offering a curated selection of shoes, from elegant heels and comfortable sneakers to durable boots and more. Our product range includes:

  • Heels: Elevate your look with our stunning heels, perfect for special occasions and formal events.
  • Sneakers: Embrace comfort and style with our trendy sneaker collection, suitable for various occasions.
  • Boots: Stay warm and fashionable with our range of boots, from ankle boots to knee-high options.
